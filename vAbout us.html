Who We Are

Founded in 2022, Justice For All is a youth-led initiative that grew out of student and community efforts to make legal knowledge available to ordinary people. We began by running legal literacy workshops in barangays and university campuses, then partnered with local NGOs and pro bono lawyers to offer free clinics.

Our work is rooted in the belief that law should protect everyone. We focus on practical services: information, simple legal advice, referrals, and community organizing to address systemic problems.

Meet Our Team

Our volunteers include law students, lecturers, community workers, and IT volunteers who manage outreach. We combine legal knowledge with grassroots organizing to ensure communities can access help and hold power accountable.

  • Law Students — research, clinics, and case intake
  • Educators — workshops and curriculum
  • Community Volunteers — outreach and organizing
  • Legal Partners — pro bono representation

Mission & Vision

Mission: Empower communities through legal education and accessible legal aid.

Vision: A Philippines where every person has fair access to justice and institutions protect rights.

Our Journey

  • 2022: Founded by university students; first legal literacy workshops conducted.
  • 2023: Expanded partnerships with NGOs and PAO; community legal clinics launched.
  • 2024: Social media campaigns and online workshops introduced; reached over 10,000 beneficiaries.
  • 2025: Consolidated programs into a national youth-led advocacy network.

Our Achievements

  • Provided legal assistance to over 2,000 individuals across 10 cities.
  • Conducted more than 50 community workshops and school seminars.
  • Partnered with 30 pro bono lawyers for free legal representation.
  • Launched awareness campaigns reaching 50,000+ people on social media.

Programs Overview

We run several programs to bring justice closer to the people:

  • Legal Literacy: Workshops and seminars for students and community members.
  • Free Legal Aid: Partner clinics and pro bono lawyers assist low-income families.
  • Anti-Corruption Campaigns: Raising awareness and providing reporting tools.
  • Online Resources: Access to guides, FAQs, and educational videos.
