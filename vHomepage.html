Justice For All

Who We Are

Justice For All is a youth-led advocacy group (founded 2022) working across the Philippines to promote access to justice, legal literacy, and stronger institutions. We partner with law students, civil-society groups, and public legal-aid providers to bring practical legal help and information to communities that need it most.

Our work began with community legal literacy sessions and grew into a broader program that includes free clinics, workshops, and campaigns against abuse and corruption. We focus on real impact — helping people understand their rights and find legal assistance when they need it.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower communities with legal knowledge and accessible legal services.

Vision: A society where justice is fair, equal, and accessible to all citizens, regardless of background.

Why This Matters

When people cannot access legal help, injustices go unchallenged. This undermines democracy, leaves vulnerable groups exposed, and weakens institutions that should protect citizens. Strengthening access to justice supports safer, fairer communities.
